# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

In the interest of fostering an open and professional environment, contributors and maintainers commit to making participation in this project respectful, constructive, and harassment-free.

## Expected Behavior

- Use respectful and inclusive language.
- Engage in evidence-based, good-faith discussion.
- Accept constructive feedback professionally.
- Focus critique on ideas, methods, and implementation rather than individuals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or hostile communication.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeated disruptive behavior that undermines collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. They may take appropriate corrective action in response to misconduct, including moderation of discussions or contribution restrictions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to maintainers with sufficient context for review.
